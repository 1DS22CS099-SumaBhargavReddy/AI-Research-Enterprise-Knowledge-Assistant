Machine learning is a subset of artificial intelligence that enables systems to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed. It focuses on the development of computer programs that can access data and use it to learn for themselves. The process begins with observations or data, such as examples, direct experience, or instruction, in order to look for patterns in data and make better decisions in the future. Deep learning is a subset of machine learning that uses neural networks with many layers. Transformers are a type of deep learning architecture that has revolutionized natural language processing.
